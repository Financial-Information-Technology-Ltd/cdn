Policy Details

This screen shows current details of a policy. There are links to other policy-related information

In the header bar, you can click on Coverages, Life Assured or Links to junmp to the relevant place on the screen (e.g. clicking Links takes you to the hyperlinks at the bottom of the page).

For client names, the family name is shown in bold.

Tip: when clicking a hyperlink, it is often useful to right click and open in a new tab, so you can have multiple tabs with policy-related information open concurrently.
