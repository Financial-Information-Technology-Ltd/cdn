Plan Premium Holiday

Parameters to determine how premium holidays are administered. Premium holidays are effected by a status change, and billing is suspended for the duration of the holiday while the policy remains active. Billing automatically restarts at the end of the holiday period, and the status is changed back to active by an ASC future record, processed by batch job LFS.FORWARD.STATUS.

Premium holidays are typically offered on savings policies, but not on protection policies.
