Plan Bonuses

Parameters relating to bonuses paid on policies.

Label

Description

Reversionary Bonus Rates

Reversionary bonus is applicable for with-profit policies. It is usually calculated annually, or bi-annually in arrears. It is paid to the policy owner when the policy matures.

This field holds the Rate table code for Reversionary Bonus. The rate tables are maintained using Support Files > Rates > Bonus Rates

Reversionary Bonus on Surrender

Identifies how much of the accumulated reversionary bonuses are paid if the policy is surrendered. Options are:

  • Pay in full
  • Proportion based on duration/term

Interim Bonus Rates

Interim bonus is usually calculated for the period from the last reversionary bonus up to the maturity/surrender date.

This field holds the Rate table code for Interim Bonus. The rate tables are maintained using Support Files > Rates > Bonus Rates

Terminal Bonus Type

A terminal bonus may be paid when the policy matures. This field identifies the calculation routine to be used for terminal bonus.

Terminal Bonus Rate Table

Rate table for Terminal Bonus. Maintained using:

Support Files > Rates > Rate Structure

Support Files > Rates > Rate Entry

Investor Bonus Type

Select the type of bonus paid on maturity or surrender of with profit or bonus guaranteed unit-linked funds from the dropdown list

Options are:

         4 - With Profits Investment Plan

         5 - With Profits Bond

Investor Bonus retained charge (%)

Percentage of the calculated investor bonus retained by the company

Investor Bonus retained limit (%)

Limit of the calculated investor bonus retained by the company
