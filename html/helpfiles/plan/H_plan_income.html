Plan Income

LIFEfit supports the automated payment of regular income from savings policies. Income payments are funded by a deduction from the savings pot. This works for both unit-linked savings (units are cancelled to fund the payment) and deposit administration (interest-bearing) savings (policys savings value is reduced to fund the payment).

Income payments are set up at new business, or may be added to a policy post-issue. They result in an RIP future record which is processed by batch job LFB.FUTURE.RIP. The details of a policys income payment(s) can be seen from a hyperlink on the policy workbench.

RIP Report Period

Period in months after the current date, up to which reporting on the effect of regular income payments should occur. This is used to determine whether the policy value is sufficient to support future income payments for this period (i.e. is the money about to "run out"?).
