Plan Unit Linked Base Details

Basic details for unit-linked investment products.

Label

Description

Plan Code

Plan Code display only

Plan Name

Plan Name display only

Fund Codes

Select the unit-linked investment funds available for the product. These will be presented for selection at new business, switch and redirection.

Fund

Select a unit-linked code from the dropdown list.

The list is comprised of fund codes defined at Support > Codes > Fund Codes.

Allocation rate table

If the fund has its own table for allocation rates (percentage of premium paid that is invested), select the table here from the dropdown list.

The list is comprised of rate tables defined at Support > Rates > Scheme Rates.

If left blank, the default allocation rules for the plan will be used (defined on screen Product Workbench > Fund Allocation).

Min %

Enter the minimum percentage that must be invested in the fund (this is validated at new business entry).

Max %

Enter the maximum percentage that may be invested in the fund .(this is validated at new business entry).

Setup Fee %

Enter the fund setup fee. This fee is taken at unit allocation.

Commission %

Enter the agent commission paid at unit allocation. Often related to the setup fee % above the difference between the figures is net income for the insurance company.

Portfolios

Fund portfolios represent a pre-defined set of fund codes to be used for a policy. They are defined at Support > Codes > Fund Portfolios.

Select fund portfolios that are available for this product. These will be presented for selection at new business, switch and redirection, rather than a selection of individual unit-linked fund codes.

Price Data Basis

The price data basis determines the relationship between the payment of premium and the timing of the resulting trigger to invest the premium received (UPP future record).

For each mode/method combination available, selected from the dropdown list, select the corresponding price data basis from the second dropdown list.

Option are:

         1 - Premium due date or if mode monthly then 1st day of the next month

         2 - Premium due date

         3 - System date

         4 - Transaction Effective Date

         5 - Premium due date + 1

         6 - 1st=Issue Date+1, Other Prems=later of prem due dt+1, prem recd dt+1

         7 - 1st=Issue Date+7, Other Prems=later of prem due dt+7, prem recd dt+7

         8 - Policy Inception Date

         9 - Later of premium due date, premium received date

         10 - Transaction Effective Date - 1 working day

Template for Automatic Fund

Some policies, typically personal portfolio bonds, have their own dedicated unit-linked fund created on policy creation. The fund is created with the same key as the policy number.

For this type of product, enter the unit-linked fund code to be used as a template for a policys dedicated fund.

Minimum Investment Value

Enter the minimum amount that can be invested in a single fund. This is validated at new business entry.

Minimum Investment Percent

Enter the minimum percentage of premium that can be invested in a single fund. This is validated at new business entry.

Months to Accumulation

For products which use initial and accumulation units, enter the number of months after which regular premiums are changed from allocating (buying) initial units to allocating accumulation units.

Initial units are subject to an extra charge, which can be accounted for up-front using actuarial funding.

Initial Unit Transfer Timing

Select the timing of the transfer of initial units to accumulation units from the dropdown list.

Options are:

         0 - No Transfer

         1 - Normal Retirement Age (typically used for pension products)

         2 - After Months to Accumulation (typically used for single premium products which use initial and accumulation units)

The transfer charge is triggered by future record ITA, processed by batch job LFS.ITA.

Initial Unit Charge Percent

Enter the initial unit charge percentage.

Initial unit charge is triggered by future record UIC, processed by batch job LFSUAL3.

Initial Unit Charge Frequency

Select the initial unit charge frequency from the dropdown list.

Options are:

         M - monthly

         Q - quarterly

         S - semi-annually

         A - annually

Initial Unit Charge Incident

Select whether initial unit charge is taken in advance or arrears. This will determine the date of the first UIC future record, created on policy issue.

Investment limits at policy issue should be used (unless changed)

Tick if the investment limits defined above (Minimum Investment Value and Minimum Investment Percent) should be applied post-issue.

Cash fund for death and maturity

Select the cash fund into which investments should be switched at death notification or maturity, in advance of death settlement or maturity settlement. This removes any investment risk where the company is obliged to pay out policy value at death notification or maturity date.
