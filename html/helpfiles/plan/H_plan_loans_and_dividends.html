Plan Loans

LIFEfit can administer policy loans (where the loan amount is paid out to the policyholder), automatic premium loans (where the loan is used to pay overdue premium in order to maintain cover) and loans to pre-finance premium tax. Loan parameters for the plan are defined on this screen.

Policy loans are created from a button on the policy maintenance screen. They are often used in conjunction with non unit-linked savings policies (i.e. endowment or deposit administration types), to enable the policyholder to access some of their savings without having to surrender the policy. For unit-linked savings, partial withdrawal functionality does a similar job.

Loan interest is capitalised (added to the outstanding loan balance) automatically by use of an INT future record which is processed by batch job LF.PROCESS.LOAN.INTEREST.

On claim (death, surrender or maturity) the claim amount is automatically reduced by the outstanding loan balance i.e. the loan is paid off. In addition, loans may be repaid in part or in full while the policy is still active.

When loan interest is posted

Select whether loan interest is calculated in advance or in arrears from the dropdown list.

If in advance, the first INT future record will be created on the day the loan is issued. If in arrears, it will be created one loan interest posting frequency after the day the loan is issued.

Number of months before loans become available

If policy loans are only available once a policy has been in force for a certain length of time, enter the number of months here.

The policy loan button will only become visible on the policy maintenance screen after this time.
