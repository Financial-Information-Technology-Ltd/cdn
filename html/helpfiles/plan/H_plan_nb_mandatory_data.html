Plan New Business Mandatory Data

Select the data fields that must be completed at pre-sales (eSurance) quote stage, new business entry and policy issue.

         If At Quote, is ticked, eSurance will not produce a pre-sales quotation (screen or document) if not completed. A red warning, advising incomplete fields, is shown at the top of the screen.

         If At New Business is ticked, a policy will be changed to its default incomplete status following new business entry if the field is empty. Again a red warning, advising incomplete fields, is shown at the top of the screen.

         If At Issue is ticked, a policy cannot be changed to an active status if the field is empty. Again a red warning, advising incomplete fields, is shown at the top of the screen.
