Mode Method Factors

Enter the factor to be used in converting an annual premium to a modal premium, for this premium frequency.

This is a way of loading premiums based on premium frequency, to reflect the higher cost of administering regular premiums.

If left blank, the modal premium will be 1/n of the annual premium, where n is the number of payments in a year (e.g. 1/12 for monthly payments)

e.g. a factor of 0.1 for monthly premiums will result in a premium of 1/10 th of the annual, as opposed to the default of 1/12 th .

Premium calculation code

Select the premium calculation code from the dropdown list.

Options are:

       0 - Sum Assured * Rate

       1 - Base coverage Sum Assured * Rate

       2 - Base premium * Rate

       3 - Return amount from rate table

       4 - Face Amount Value * Rate

       5 - Base coverage Face Amount Value * Rate

       7 - Dutch term product level premium

       8 - Dutch term product changing premium

       13 - Multiplier Based

       14 - Sum Assured * Rate * (1-Additional Rate)

       16 - Rate Per Mille of Face Amount

       17 - Averaged premium on loan

       19 - Percentage of the premium on all other coverages

       22 - New Business only calc SA = Premium - (Rate table value 1 * modal factor)* 10000/(Rate table value 2 * modal factor), times duration

       23 - Sum Assured * Rate table value 1 + Rateable value 2

       24 - Premiums to be cover plus Policy Fee * Rate

       25 - Leadway term premium calculation

       26 - VLBM premium calculation

       27 - Sum Assured * Rate unless premium endorsement then (Pt / Po ) * Sa

       28 - JL rider Sum Assured * Term(months) * Rate

       29 - Leadway FBP from first principles

       30 - Leadway FBPL2 from first principles

Recalculate premium every time

Tick if the premium due should be recalculated on every premium payment. This is often used where the premium is age-dependent, and should increase on each policy anniversary.

Premium Tax

Select the premium tax processing method from the dropdown list.

Options are:

       1 - Deduct tax from premium

       2 - Account for the tax without deduction from premium

Please note that the premium tax rate is country-specific, and set at Support > Country > Country.

New Business Tax

Select the new business tax processing method from the dropdown list (tax on policy issue as a percentage of the sum assured sometimes paid as a levy to the regulatory authority).

Options are:

       1 - Deduct tax from premium

       2 - Account for the tax without deduction from premium

Please note that the new business tax rate is country-specific, and set at Support > Country > Country.

Return unused premium on termination

Tick if, on termination before the policys paid-to date, a pro-rata amount of premium should be returned to the policyholder.

e.g. for an annual policy cancelled after 8 months, one third of the premium will be refunded.
