Plan Premium Waiver

Waiver of premium is often sold as an additional rider benefit. In the event the policyholder is no longer able to pay premiums, usually as a result of job loss following sickness or disability, the company waives the premiums and pays them itself.

Waiver claims are processed as a two-stage status change, waiver notification followed by waiver claim. On status change to waiver claim, a WPP future record is created for the next premium due date. This is processed by batch job LF.PAY.WAIVER.PREMIUMS which accounts for premium from a company profit & loss account; accounting pseudo WPREM replaces the cash pseudo in a cash>premium transaction. All other processing (e.g. commission, allocation, deposit administration) that takes place as a result of premium payment continues as normal.

Type of waiver of premium this product represents

Select the waiver type from the dropdown list:

Options are:

         1 - Normal waiver of premium

         2 - Waiver of premium on death

"Normal" waiver of premium is related to sickness or disability, whereby the policyholder is no longer able to pay his premiums.

LIFEfit also supports waiver of premium as a death benefit (an alternative to a lump sum payment on death). In this case, the company "pays" premiums until maturity, and the maturity value is paid out to the policy beneficiaries.
