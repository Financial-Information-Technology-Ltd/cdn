Plan Switching

Parameters for fees for switching unit-linked funds (moving money between funds). Fees are automatically calculated according to the values set here.

Enter the maximum switch fee. This will limit the percentage fee entered above.

Minimum switch fee

Enter the minimum switch fee. This will limit the percentage fee entered above.

Tip for a fixed switch fee, set the maximum and minimum to the same value.

Minimum switch value

Enter the minimum value of units that may be switched.

Free Switches allowed per year

Enter the number of switches per policy year that will be processed with no fee.

Switch Basis

Select the pricing basis for ad hoc switches from the dropdown list.

Options are:

         1 - Bid to Bid

         2 - Offer to Offer

         3 - Bid to Offer

         4 - Offer to Bid

Bid price is lower than offer price. If a switch is done bid to offer, the company makes money on the bid/offer spread (i.e. Units are switched out at bid price, and switched in at offer price).

Auto switch basis

Select the pricing basis for automatic switches from the dropdown list. Automatic switching happens where there is a phased switch of money from the money market fund used during cool-off into the selected investment funds. This can be used to smooth investment risk and is typically seen in single premium products.

Options are:

         1 - Bid to Bid

         2 - Offer to Offer

         3 - Bid to Offer

         4 - Offer to Bid

Drip Feed Fee

Enter the drip feed fee (where there is a phased switch of money from the money market fund used during cool-off into the selected investment funds)
