Trailer Commissions

Trailer commission is triggered by a UTC future record, processed by batch job LFS.TRAILER.COMMISSIONS. If set here, a UTC future record for the first trailer commission date will be created on policy issue.

Trailer commission can be a fixed percentage of policy value, a variable percentage read from a rate table, or a fixed amount, as configured below.

Trailer Comms Allowed

Tick if trailer commission is applicable to this plan.

Trailer Comms Reducable

Tick if trailer commission can be reduced at policy level (leave unticked if it is the same for all policies, set on this screen)

Incidence

Select whether trailer commission is generated in advance or in arrears, in conjunction with the frequency selected below.

Trailer Comm Frequency

Select the frequency of trailer commission from the dropdown list.

Options are:

         A - Annual

         M - Monthly

         ME - Month-End

         Q - Quarterly

         QE - Quarter End (as defined at Company level)

Don't deduct units

By default, trailer commission is deducted from policy value by cancelling units. Tick this option if policy value should be unaffected, and the commission is paid by the company.

Trailer Comms Basis

Select the trailer commission basis from the dropdown list.

Options are:

         0 - No Trailer Commissions

         1 - Rate * Policy Value

Trailer Comm Percent

Enter the fixed annual trailer commission percentage, if appropriate.

Trailer Comm Table

Select the rate table containing the annual trailer commission percentage, if appropriate, from the dropdown list.

The list is comprised of rate tables defined at Support > Rates > Scheme Rates

Trailer Comm Amount

Enter the fixed trailer commission amount, if appropriate.

Duration

Duration in years for which trailer commission is paid. After this period, no UTC future records will be generated.

If blank, trailer commission will be paid until the policy ends.

Reserve Commissions

This is used where commission is funded up-front by a reinsurer, and paid back to them over time by deduction from policy value.

Reserve commission is triggered by a PTC future record, processed by batch job LF.RESERVE.COMMISSIONS. If set here, a PTC future record for the first reserve commission date will be created on policy issue.

Reserve Comms Basis

Select the reserve commission basis from the dropdown list.

Options are:

         0 - No Trailer Commissions

         1 - Rate * Policy Value

Incidence

Select whether reserve commission is generated in advance or in arrears, in conjunction with the frequency selected below.

Reserve Comms Frequency

Select the frequency of reserve commission from the dropdown list.

Options are:

         A - Annual

         M - Monthly

         Q - Quarterly

Periodic Trailer Commission

Enter the schedule of reserve commission in table format. The percentage can vary over time
