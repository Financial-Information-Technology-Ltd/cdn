Commission Type Structures

The parameters of the commission type are defined on this screen, including the basic calculation for initial and renewal commission

If the last character of the commission type is ] (wildcard), you can click a button to set the rules for commission types which vary by policy term or sum assured band.

Renewal commission calculation code

Method of calculation renewal commission.

Renewal commission is usually paid from the end of the initial commission period until the end of the premium payment term.

Options

         (Gross Premium * Rate)

         (Net Premium * Rate)

         2nd year: (Gross Modal Premium * term); Other years: (Gross Premium * Rate)

         (Total premium * Commission rate)

Initial commissions that left to agent when terminated

Amount of commission to be left for the agent on policy termination (i.e. not clawed back)

Earnings Period (Years)

Commission earnings period

Use Effective Date to calculate duration

The policy duration, where the commission rate varies over a policys term, is usually based on a policys paid-to date.

This feature is often used for soft-billed products (e.g. deposit administration savings) where there is, in effect, no paid-to date.

e.g. pay 5% commission for premiums received in the first year, 2% for premiums received in the second year, and 1% for premiums received from the third year onwards.
