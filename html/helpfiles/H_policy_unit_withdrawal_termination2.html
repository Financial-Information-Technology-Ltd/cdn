Unit Withdrawal Quote

Shows the full breakdown of the policy surrender value, including any penalties and bonuses that apply.

Allows you to print a surrender quote document.

Label

Description

Policy No

Policy Number

Withdrawal Type

FSUR Full Surrender

Plan

Product type (policy plan code)

Policy Funds

For unit-linked policies, the funds currently held

Initial Units

For unit-linked policies, the number of initial units (typically units bought in the first two years of the policys life). Initial units may be subject to additional charges.

Accum Units

For unit-linked policies, the number of accumulation units (typically units bought after the two years of the policys life)

Fund Ccy

For unit-linked policies, the currency of the fund.

Policy Ccy Value

For unit-linked policies, the value of the units in policy currency (using the most recent exchange rate before the surrender quote date if necessary)

Reorg.Bonus

A special, fund-specific bonus which may apply to unit-linked policies. The percentage used to calculate this bonus is defined on the fund record.

Total Unit Withdrawal

For unit-linked policies, the total value of units on the policy

Total Traditional Withdrawal

For non unit-linked policies, the total policy value (Deposit Value or Endowment Surrender Value)

Withdrawal Penalty

Any penalty, calculated according to product rules (e.g. percentage of value penalty applicable in early years of a policy)

Fixed fee

Fixed surrender fee, according to product rules

Reorganisation bonus

Total special, fund-specific bonus which may apply to unit-linked policies.

Terminal bonus

Terminal bonus (or market value reduction), calculated according to product rules.

Typically based on the underlying investment performance for policies with money invested in with profits funds.

Net Withdrawal

Net withdrawal amount, after penalties and bonuses have been applied.

Withdrawal date

Surrender quotation date (display only, as entered on previous screen)

Actuarial Funding Adjustment.

For unit-linked policies featuring actuarially-funded initial units, an explanation of the difference between the policy unit value (which will have been quoted to the policyholder) and the company unit value (actual value of investments held).

On surrender, the value of company units is paid, which is lower than the value of policy units. At maturity the two types of unit are worth the same.
