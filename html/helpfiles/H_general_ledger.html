General Ledger

View LIFEfit general ledger postings, summarised by month.

Label

Description

Account No

General ledger account number, as entered on the previous screen (display only).

Company

Company code, as entered on the previous screen (display only).

Branch

Branch code, as entered on the previous screen (display only).

Currency

Currency code, as entered on the previous screen (display only).

Account Type

Select the account type from the dropdown list.

Options are:

         A - Assets

         B - Balance Sheet

         C - Claim

         E - Expense

         I - Income

         L - Liability

         M - Memo

         P - Profit & Loss

         T - Tax

This determines the order that the account will appear in the general ledger report (LF012) and trial balance (LF013). It is defaulted to the account type of the corresponding accounting pseudo code.

Account Name

Enter the account name. It is defaulted to the description of the corresponding accounting pseudo code.

Ledger Code

Select from the dropdown list.

Options are:

C Company Ledger

F Fund ledger.

This determines whether the account will be included in the nominal ledger export (some companies choose to export the company ledger information only - company and plan level accounts. Fund level detail is kept in LIFEfit for analysis)

Year

Lists years in which postings have been made.

Actual / Budget

Select Account Balances or Budget from the dropdown list. Default is Account Balances.

Year beginning balance.
