Premium Statement

Statement showing transaction details between dates selected (the reporting period).

For deposit administration savings products, includes opening and closing balances, and detail and summary of transactions of different types that affect policy value.

For endowment savings and risk only products, details of premiums received in the reporting period are shown.

Label

Description

Policy

Policy Number

Statement Start Date

Start date for statement

Defaults to 1 January of the current year, and can be changed

Statement End Date

End date for statement

Defaults to current system date, and can be changed

Opening Balance

Savings balance at statement start date

Total Payment

Total of payments received in the reporting period

Total Interest

Total savings interest credited in the reporting period

Total Withdrawal

Total of withdrawals (ad hoc withdrawals, regular income payments and full surrender) in the reporting period

Total Risk Premium

Total of mortality risk premiums paid from savings value in the reporting period

Closing Balance

Savings balance at statement end date (sum of the values above)

Date

Actual date of transaction

Effective Date

Effective date of transaction (payment transactions can be backdated)

Transaction description

Receipt no

Mode of payment

For payments, the type of payment (e.g. cash, cheque, electronic fund transfer)
