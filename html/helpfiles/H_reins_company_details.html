Reinsurance Companies

View and edit the paramters for a reinsurer.

Label

Description

Company Code

Company code, as selected or entered on the previous screen. Display only.

Name

Reinsurance company name.

Billing Address

Reinsurance company address.

Reinsurance Accounts

Accounts Payable

Select the account pseudo to be used for accounts payable. Debited on processing a reinsurance premium, triggered by a REINS future record.

Account Paid

Select the account pseudo to be used for account paid. Credited on processing a reinsurance premium, triggered by a REINS future record.

Rebate Account

Select the account pseudo to be used for reinsurance rebate, also known as reinsurance commission.

Claim Account

Select the account pseudo to be debIted on reinsurance claim (i.e. claim on a policy where some of the sum at risk is reinsured)

Advance 1 Account

Select the first account pseudo to be used when fees and charges are financed by the reinsurer.

Advance 2 Account

Select the second account pseudo to be used when fees and charges are financed by the reinsurer.

Claims Writeoff

Select the account pseudo to be used when amounts advanced are written off at claim by the reinsurer.
