Co-Insurance Company Details

This screen contains details about a life insurance company that can be involved in a coinsurance arrangement for Group Life business.

Co-Insurance is an arrangement whereby a group of insurers share the risk and premium on a Group Life scheme in an agreed proportion. One insurer takes the largest share and is nominated the leader. The other companies are designated as followers. The co-insurers on a Group Life scheme, and their proportional split of risk and premium, are defined as part of the scheme setup process.

This screen enables separation of accounting between coinsurers for reporting and aggregation.

Pseudos are defined at Support > Accounting > Pseudo Codes. You are advised to have separate pseudo codes for each coinsurer/reason, and use meaningful names to make them easy to select on the policy accounting screen.
