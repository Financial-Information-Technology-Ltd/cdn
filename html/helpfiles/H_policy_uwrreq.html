Policy Outstanding Requirements

Lists policy underwriting requirements.

If accessed from the policy maintenance screen, enables the update of policy underwriting information. Once all requirements are cleared, the policys status automatically changes.

<Add New Requirement> button

Enables you to add another underwriting requirement manually

<Set Medical Provider> button

Enables you to set the medical provider to whom the life assured is being sent for tests. Can initiate a letter or email to

-        the life assured, informing them of what tests are required and where they should be undertaken

-        the medical provider (clinic or doctor) to advise them to expect the life assured to come for tests, and which tests are required

<Set Provider Fees> button

Enables you to set the medical fee due to the provider for undertaking the tests. A batch process will initiate payment of the fees once tests are returned

<Set To Received> button

Sets the status of all underwriting requirements that have been checked (check box to left of underwriting code) to Received OK.

The received date is set to the current system date.
