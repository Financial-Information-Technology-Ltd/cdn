Unreconciled Bank Transactions

Shows details of an unreconciled bank transaction, and allows it to be reconciled with a policy or deleted

Description

Info 1

Info 2

National Insurance Number from bank statement (if used)

Reason

Payment reason

Bank Name

Bank name

Currency

Payment currency

Amount

Payment amount

Policy No

Policy number (can be edited).

Policy Owner

Policy owner name

Policy Status

Policy status

Next Premium

Next premium amount due on policy

Reconcile transaction to policy
