Label

Description

Policy

Policy Number

Coverage

Policy coverage number

Restrict payments

Are annuity payments prevented

Plan

Plan code and plan name

Active Annuity

Contains X if annuity is currently in payment

Coverage Status

Status of policy coverage

Owner

Policy owner

Coverage Issue Date

Issue date of policy coverage

Funded Policy No

If this annuity is being used to fund premiums on a separate policy, the number of the policy being funded

Frequency

Annuity payment frequency

Term Years

Annuity term in years

Term Months

Annuity term in months (in addition to the whole years)

Term Number

For annuities with a fixed number of payments, the number of payments

First Pay Date

First annuity payment date

Final Pay Date

Final annuity payment date (for whole of life annuities, such as most pension annuities, this is blank and the annuity is paid until death)

Guaranteed End Date

For annuities with a guaranteed minimum term, the final guaranteed payment date.

This is typically used for pension annuities.

e.g. an annuity with a guaranteed minimum term of five years: the annuity will be paid until this date to the nominated death beneficiary if the annuitant dies within five years of buying the annuity

Gross Modal Annuity

Amount of each annuity payment before tax

Net Modal Annuity

Amount of each annuity payment after tax

Previous Payment Date

Most recent annuity paid date

Previous Payment Amount

Most recent annuity amount paid

Next Payment Date

Next annuity payment due date

Next Payment Amount

Next annuity payment amount

Tax Basis

Taxation basis

PAYE Tax Code

Tax code

Escalation Type

Type of annuity escalation (if any)

Escalation Rate

Annuity escalation rate

Reversionary Percentage

Percentage of annuity the will be paid to the spouse, in the event of the annuitants death.

Often seen for pension annuities.

Different reversionary percentages (typically 50%, 66.67%, 75% and 100%) can be offered at new business. Selecting a reversionary annuity results in a lower initial annuity.

Payment Method

Method of annuity payment

e.g. cheque, direct credit

Payee No

Client number of the annuity recipient

Bank

For annuities paid by direct credit, the bank name and bank account number which is credited

Payee Name

Name of the annuity recipient
