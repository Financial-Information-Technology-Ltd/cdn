Payment Out Reason

This screen enables you to define the cash accounting pseudo to be used for a type of payment out transaction.

This allows you, for example, to map to different bank accounts for making claim payments, commission payments and annuity payments.

The accounting that takes place when a claim payment is processed is

dr <claim reason pseudo>

cr <payment method pseudo>

dr <payment method pseudo>

cr <payment out cash pseudo> (defined here)

e.g. for a medical underwriting expense, paid by direct credit to the clinic that performed the test

dr MED_FEE_PEND

cr DCRED

dr DCRED

cr <payment out cash pseudo> (defined here)
