Policy Activity

This is the screen where all the financial transactions that have taken place on a policy are shown, such as premium payments, premium reversals, interest and claims.

By default, the list is shown in reverse order of effective date (most recent at the top).

The list can be re-sorted by any of its columns. One click sorts the list, and a second click reverses the sort order.

You can click on a transaction to see further detail.

Label

Description

Effective Date

The system effective date of the transaction

Trans.No

Transaction number

Ccy

Transaction currency

Transaction

Transaction reason e.g. premium payment or reversal

Cash

Amount posted to Cash

Premium

Amount posted to Premium

Suspense

Amount posted to Suspense

User

The User ID of the person that made the transaction

Paid to Date

For premium payments, the paid-to date (the end of the period covered by the premium - when the next premium is due)

Rv (Reversal)

An asterisk (*) appears here for transactions that have been subsequently reversed, and for reversal transactions themselves
