Process Maturity

Allows an investment fund to mature. The fund house provides a total maturity amount and this is split across all the policies in proportion to their holdings.

<Process Maturity> button

Process the fund maturity.

Fund units are sold and an FMAT future record is created for allocation of the maturity amount into a cash fund defined at Admin > Config > System flags. The following batch process needs to be run to process FMAT future record LF.PROCESS.FUND.MATURITY.

Two accounting pseudos represent the maturity amount:

FDMAT - mapped at fund level (fund level margin pseudo)

PFMAT - mapped at plan level (plan level margin pseudo)
