New Business Annuity Screen 2

Screen Description

Label

Description

Policy Details

Start Date

The Effective Date of the Annuity. Annuity payment dates will be based on this date. If the Effective Date should be the date that the premium is received, then leave this date blank. If blank, it will be set to the System Date when the policy is activated.

Input Annuity Amount

Controls if the Annuity Amount or the Premium should be entered. The other value will be calculated by the system.

Premium

The cost of purchasing the Annuity. This is a lump-sum payment, usually from a pension.

Annuity Frequency

The mode of payment of the Annuity Amount

Annuity Amount

The modal payment amount.

Spouse Reversionary Percentage

For Joint Life Policies, enter the percentage payable (if any) to the Second Life on the

death of the First Life. For example, if the Spouse is to receive half the Annuity Pension on the Death of the Annuitant, set this field to 50.00%. An entry of 0.00% means regular payments will normally cease on the death of the First Life (though payments may continue until the end of any Guaranteed Term).

Escalation Percentage

The Annuity Amount will be increased by this rate each anniversary.

Guaranteed Period (months)

The Annuity Payments are guaranteed for this period even if the Annuitant dies.

Source Of Premium

Pension Fund Administrator

Bank Details

Payment Method

Payment Method of the Annuity Amount
