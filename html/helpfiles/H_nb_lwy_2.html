New Business - Policy Details

Step 2 of new business is where the main details of the product are entered e.g. term, premium, payment method. This screen will vary depending upon the type of policy to be effective but a basic set of data will be required for every policy type. Any Inputs are validated against the particular rules for the product being sold, such as minimum term of policy or face amount. If the rules are not adhered to, error messages appear at the top of the screen so that these have to be addressed before the use can proceed to next screen.

Label

Description

Policy Details

Application Date

The Date which the application was received.

Sum Assured

A mandatory field which differs from product to product. The Sum Assured that is entered should be within the limit set at Plans > Basic > General

In the case of the Comfort 5 product where a policy holder is able to combine multiple policies. A customer will 'combine multiple policies' by selecting a sum assured that is a multiple of the basic sum assured. The permitted sums assured are defined in a multi-value field at Plans > New Business. This populates a dropdown box for selecting the sum assured in the new business screen:

For the Targeted Savings Plan, this is the Target amount.

Payment Frequency

Mandatory field which is subject to product settings. Typically a Drop Down Box is displayed for which available frequencies are defined at Plan Level.

Commencement Date

Start date for the policy coverage

Payment Method

A drop down list of methods of premium payment. The payment methods options are:

  • Cash/Cheque/EFT
  • Direct Debit

If a payment method of Direct debit is chosen, additional fields are displayed on the screen to capture the bank details.

Bank Details

Sort code

Enter bank sort code

Bank Name

Enter the name of the bank

Branch Name

Enter branch of the bank

Account Number

Enter Bank Account Number

Account Name

Enter Account Name (automatically defaults policy owner name/surname)

Term (display only)

This field is mandatory and subject to Product Setup. (Plans > Basic > Maturity)

With the Comfort 5 Product the Policy term is a display only field as it is set at New Business > Defaults

Medical Questionnaire

A series of medical questions is displayed upon new business application most of which are in the form of Yes/No, Integer or Text type.

For a YES/NO question, if YES is checked, further fields are displayed on the screen. For an Integer Type Question LIFE fit will only permit numerical characters. If any other characters are keyed, LIFE fit will display an error message.

Height

Height can either be entered in Cm or Ft and In. If the Height is entered in Cm LIFE fit calculates this height into Ft and Inches which is automatically displayed on the screen.

Weight

Weight can either be entered in Kg or Ib. If the Weight is entered in Kg, then LIFEfit calculates the weight in pounds which is automatically displayed on the screen.
