System Flags

Various system configuration parameters.

hint: it is good practice, after changing any of the parameters on this screen, to to a /serguei (i.e. enter /serguei at the end of the LIFEfit url in the address bar above the LIFEfit screen). This ensures that any cached settings are cleared, and the changes take effect.

Label

Description

Key

LIFEfit system key, as configured at system installation (display only).

Accounting Basis

Select the unit-linked accounting basis from the dropdown list. Options are

1 Standard

2 Company units

The different bases involve different sets of accounting pseudos for unit-linked transactions. For more information about company unit accounting, please see Support > Product Workbench, Unit Linked Pre-funding.

Funded Initial Units

Tick if actuarially-funded initial units are used. Again you can find more detail about this at Support > Product Workbench, Unit Linked Pre-funding

Standard Price Margin

If ticked, bare-bid margin postings will be made (number of units * difference between bare price and bid price).

Include Fluctuation Accounting

Tick if, for backdated unit-linked transactions, an extra posting should be made to account for the difference (fluctuation) in prices between the price used and the current price. This represents a profit (or loss) to the insurance company.

Multiple Cash Books

Tick if multiple cash books (multiple cash pseudos) are used. These represent incoming and outgoing bank accounts, and are important for reconciling cash balances at the bank with LIFEfit cash account balances.

If ticked, anywhere in LIFEfit where you make a cash accounting posting, you will be asked to select the cash pseudo (i.e. bank account) to which it relates. Examples are the policy accounting screen, and the cash receipting screen.

Pricing Basis

Select the unit-linked pricing basis from the dropdown list. Options are:

       0 - Backward Price (system looks back in time from the transaction date for a price, within the backdate limits defined. This is unusual as the company is taking a pricing risk, buying units at an old price).

       1 - Forward Price (system looks forward in time from the transaction date for a price. This is more usual as the policyholder is taking a pricing risk, and units are bough at the market price at the time of purchase)

       2 - Dealing Day Pricing (system creates triggers for buying/selling, such as allocation and surrender, based on the next day funds involved in the transaction will be priced. Similar to forward pricing, but avoids attempting to process transactions on days when prices will not be available).

Forward backdate limit (days)

Limits the number of days a unit switch instruction can be backdated.

Prevent Duplicate Client Creation

Tick if LIFEfit should warn on creating a client where the name and date of birth match those of an existing client record.

Process to import bank transactions

Select the bank transaction import process from the dropdown list.

ID Number check

Select the client ID number check (if any) from the dropdown list. This is designed to prevent mis-keying of ID numbers on the client workbench. Options, which may be extended in the future, are:

       NL - Burgerservicenummer (Nederland) - Dutch Citizens Service Number.

       POL - Policy PESEL check - Polish "Powszechny Elektroniczny System Ewidencji Ludnosci", Universal Electronic System for Registration of the Population, an 11 digit unique identifier used since 1979 for all permanent residents of Poland and for temporary residents living in Poland for over 2 months.

       ROM - Romanian CND check - the individual's Cod Numeric Personal (CNP), or Personal Numeric Code, a 13 digit unique identifying number issued to every Romanian citizen at 14 years of age.

Unique Application Number Check

Where applications are numbered by an external application, ensure that the number is unique when keyed-in (or imported) to LIFEfit.

Pseudo for reversing unreconciled bank transactions

On reversing an unreconciled transaction (Premiums/Payments > Unreconciled Transaction, delete transaction), the pseudo which will be credited to balance the debit on pseudo MISCSUSP.

Policy and unit accounting get to GENACCTRN immediately

Tick if policy an unit accounting journal entries (records in files POLACCTRN and UNITACCTRN) should be copied to the consolidated journal (file GENACCTRN) immediately on creation, rather than in the end of day accounting batch process. Usually used in conjunction with the real time export of accounting data to the general ledger.

Payment Out Currencies

Cheque Payment

Select the currencies in which cheque payments out may be made from the dropdown list.

The list is comprised of currencies flagged as active (Support > Currency > Currency Codes).

These will be given as options when creating a payment out (e.g. death claim, partial surrender) which is to be paid by cheque.

Direct Credit

Select the currencies in which direct credit payments out may be made from the dropdown list.

The list is comprised of currencies flagged as active (Support > Currency > Currency Codes).

These will be given as options when creating a payment out (e.g. death claim, partial surrender) which is to be paid by direct credit.

Structured Note:

Structured Note currency

Select a currency from the dropdown list.

Structured Note policy

Structured notes are funds which can only be purchased in block amounts e.g. multiples of 1,000. Where total value required (across all investing clients) is not divisible by the block size, the amount required to round up the total client allocation to the next block multiple will be invested in this policy, owned by the insurance company.

Structured Note Cash Funds

Cash fund into which dividends on structured notes should be paid, and into which maturing structured notes should be switched.
