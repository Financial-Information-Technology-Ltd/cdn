Compliance Workbench

LIFEfit supports automatic checking of its entire client database against a variety of online resources.

The locations of these lists on the web are changed from time to time, so they are parameterised on LIFEfit.

Batch jobs perform the checks, and email the nominated compliance officer, identifying possible matches or confirming no matches have been found. They are typically included as part of a daily batch run.

Label

Description

Financial Sanctions Check Database

This list is published by the UK Treasury Department

It typically holds details of known and suspected terrorists and terrorist organisations, and officials of governments of rogue states.

Politically Exposed Persons (PEP) Check Database

This is a list of heads of state and government ministers maintained by the CIA

OFAC of US Treasury list URL

This list is maintained by the Office of Foreign Assets Control of the US Treasury (it is a similar list to that maintained by the UK Treasury).

Checking against this list forms part of compliance with the USA PATRIOT Act 2001.

USA PATRIOT stands for Uniting (and) Strengthening America (by) Providing Appropriate Tools Required (to) Intercept (and) Obstruct Terrorism.

EU Sanctions List URL

This is the European Union version of the UK and US Treasury lists.

Internal Sanctions List File Path

You can maintain your own sanctions check list, in the same format as the EU Sanctions list.
