Payment Out

This screen enables you to make an ad hoc payment to an agent, deducted from his/her commission balance. It is used to enable commission to be paid put without waiting until the next commission statement date.

Payments will need to go through standard 4-eye authorisation checking (Premium/Payments > Payments Out Authorisation), and will appear in the next commission statement
