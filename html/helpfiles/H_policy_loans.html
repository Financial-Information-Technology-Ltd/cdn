Policy Loans

Details of any loans taken out on the policy.

Loans are often available on traditional (i.e. non unit-linked) savings products, subject to certain restrictions (e.g. maximum amount, minimum amount, policy duration). They enable the policyholder to release funds, while keeping the policy active. Partial surrenders perform a similar function for unit-linked savings products.

Interest accrues, and outstanding loan balances will be deducted from the benefit amount paid on claim (surrender, death, maturity).

Loans can be repaid in part or full.
