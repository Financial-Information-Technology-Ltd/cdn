Underwriting Requirement

Shows details of underwriting requirements applied to the policy.

This is a display-only enquiry screen. Its details are updated from a button on the policy maintenance screen.

Label

Description

Policy Number

Policy Number

Coverage

Policy coverage to which the underwriting requirement applies

Outstanding Code

Underwriting requirement code and description

Client

Number and name of client to whom the underwriting requirement applies.

In brackets, the role(s) this client plays on the policy

Operator

The user who created the underwriting requirement

In the case of requirements triggered automatically by product rules at new business (e.g. sum assured above a certain threshold), the user who keyed-in the policy at new business

Date requested

Date the underwriting requirement was applied

Follow up days

Number of days after creation of the requirement that, if the requirement is still outstanding, an automatic chaser can be sent.

Set as part of the rules of the particular underwriting requirement.

Can be used to trigger a letter or email.

Next Follow up

Scheduled date of next follow up

Last Change Date

Date of most recent change to the underwriting requirement

Changed by

User who made the most recent change to the underwriting requirement

Last followed up

Date of most recent follow up

Generated

How the requirement was generated (System or Manual)

Comment

Comment free format text

Date Reply Received

Date the underwriters decision was received

Status

Underwriting requirement status

Medical Provider

Medical provider assigned to carry out the underwriting test

Medical invoice

Invoice number received from the medical provider

Providers Fee

Fee paid to medical provider for carrying out the medical test

Date Fee Paid

Date the medical providers fee was paid
