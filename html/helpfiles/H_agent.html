Persistency

Represents the proportion of business sold by the agent that remains active (i.e. has not lapsed) for a company-defined period after issue.

Can be used in conjunction with parameters on Support > Organisation > Company to determine how much indemnified commission should be clawed-back in the event of a lapse.

The idea is to claw back more commission from agents who produce poor quality business, with lots of lapses due to non-payment, and reward those for whom most of the policies they sell are maintained as premium paying.

Numeric (2 decimal places).
