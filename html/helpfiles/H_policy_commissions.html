Policy Commission Details

Shows details of commission payments and earnings for a policy coverage

Label

Description

Policy No

Policy Number (display only)

Coverage

Select a policy coverage from the dropdown.

Commission payment and earnings are managed at coverage level, and each coverage (benefit) has its own rules

Initial Commission Entitlement

Total commission due in the coverages initial commission period

Commission Sacrifice

Amount of commission given up by the agent, usually in return for a lower premium or higher unit allocation rate

Initial Commission Sacrifice

Amount of initial commission given up by the agent, usually in return for a lower premium or higher unit allocation rate

Renewal Commission Sacrifice

Amount of renewal commission given up by the agent, usually in return for a lower premium or higher unit allocation rate

Agent code

List of agent codes of writing and override agents who will receive commission on the coverage

Agent name

List of names of writing and override agents who will receive commission on the coverage

Balance to Date Earned

Amount of commission earned by the agent (commission is earned when premium is received)

Balance to Date - Unearned

Amount of commission yet to be earned by the agent (commission can be indemnified, i.e. paid in advance, and earned as premium are paid)

Balance to Date Paid

Amount of commission paid to the agent (sum of earned and unearned amounts)

Renewal Agents Agent Code

List of agent codes of agents who will receive renewal commission on the coverage

Renewal Agents Agent name

List of names of agents who will receive renewal commission on the coverage

Commission Rate of Annualised premium

Commission rate (as a percentage of premium received) by policy year.

Companies often pay a higher percentage in the early years.

Pattern code

Commission pattern code, as defined in the product rules.

The agents commission schedule, and the products commission pattern code determine how commission will be paid and earned for the coverage.

Base Commission Rate

The standard commission rate, set according to the pattern code and agent type

Commission Enhancement

Commission enhancement, if any, set on coverage level and entered at new business if applicable

Allocation Enhancement

Enhancement to standard allocation rate, if any. Usually as a result of commission sacrificed by the agent

Trailer commission

Renewal commission on unit-linked policies, paid as a percentage of policy value.

Can be funded by cancelling policy units

Allocation rate

Unit-linked allocation rate
