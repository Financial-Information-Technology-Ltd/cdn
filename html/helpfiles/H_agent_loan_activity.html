Agent Loan History

This screen shows the transaction history for the selected agent loan

Label

Description

Loan Amount

The original loan amount.

Loan Term

The original Term in Months of the loan.

Interest Rate

The effective interest rate for the loan.

Loan Issue Date

The effective date of the loan advance

Repayment Frequency

This is used to calculate the date of the next loan repayment. This date is held on the LOANA future record.

Repayment Amount

The modal repayment amount. This is calculated when the loan is issued. Additional repayments do not alter the modal repayment out, they reduce the term of the loan.

Final Payment Date

The date of the final loan repayment. This is calculated using the Loan Issue Date and Loan Term. It is not re-calculated if additional repayments reduce the term of the loan.

Transaction History:

Date

The effective date of the transaction

Repayment

The repayment amount. For a regular modal loan repayment deducted from the agents commission, this will consist of a capital amount plus an interest amount. For an additional repayment, this value will be all capital.

Outstanding Balance

The capital amount remaining on the loan.

Statement

The commission statement number containing the repayment.
