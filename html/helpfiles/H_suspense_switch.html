Policy Suspense Switch

This screen is used to move money from suspense on one policy to another. It is usually used when money has accidentally been credited to the wrong policy. The amount of money in suspense can be seen on the policy workbench

Label

Description

From policy

The policy that has been incorrectly credited (the source policy). For information, the policy owner's name is displayed.

Suspense (display only)

The amount of money currently in suspense on the source policy.

To policy

The policy that should have been credited, and is to receive the money (the target policy). Again, the owner's name is displayed for information.

Amount

The amount of money to be moved from the source policy to the target policy. This cannot be more than the amount in suspense on the source policy.
