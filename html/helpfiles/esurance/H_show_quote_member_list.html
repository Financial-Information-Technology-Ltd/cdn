Add a new scheme member by screen input

<Import Members> button

Import scheme members from a CSV file

Format of the CSV is

Quotation No :,<Scheme number>,,<count>

Employee No,Title,Forenames,Surname,Gender (M or F),Marital status,Date of Birth (dd/mm/yyyy),Salary (2dp),Id Number,Subgroup,Address,City (LGA),Phone No,Email Address,Date of Joining Scheme  (dd/mm/yyyy),Date of Joining Company  (dd/mm/yyyy),Date of Leaving Scheme  (dd/mm/yyyy),Change Date  (dd/mm/yyyy)

Quotation No is scheme number as shown above.

<count> enables multiple CSVs to be imported for the same quotation (it should start at 1, and be incremented each time)

e.g.

Quotation No :,137*0,,2,,,,,,,,,,,,,,

Employee No,Title,Forenames,Surname,Gender (M or F),Marital status,Date of Birth (dd/mm/yyyy),Salary (2dp),Id Number,Subgroup,Address,City (LGA),Phone No,Email Address,Date of Joining Scheme  (dd/mm/yyyy),Date of Joining Company  (dd/mm/yyyy),Date of Leaving Scheme  (dd/mm/yyyy),Change Date  (dd/mm/yyyy)

n.b. you can produce a first stage quotation with minimal member data (employee number, date of birth and salary). In order to submit the scheme to the back office for processing, a second stage quote must be produced containing further details according to product rules (usually forenames, surname, address, LGA)

Columns in CSV more detail:

-        Quotation No :n*m (where n is scheme number, and m is quote counter for the scheme).

-        Count - enables multiple CSVs to be imported for the same quotation (it should start at 1, and be incremented each time)

-        Employee No - staff number

-        Title - MISS, MRS, MS, DR, OTHER

-        Forenames - free format text

-        Surname - free format text

-        Gender (M or F) - M (male), F (female)

-        Marital status - M (married), S (single), W (widow), W2 (widower), D (divorced), S2 (separated)

-        Date of Birth (dd/mm/yyyy)

-        Salary (2dp) - Annual salary. Numeric, with two decimal places if required

-        Id Number - free format text

-        Subgroup - number of subgroup to which the member belongs

-        Address - street address (free format text)

-        City (LGA) LGA name

-        Phone No free format text

-        Email Address free format text

-        Date of Joining Scheme (dd/mm/yyyy) optional

-        Date of Joining Company (dd/mm/yyyy) optional

-        Date of Leaving Scheme (dd/mm/yyyy) - not used at quote stage

-        Change Date (dd/mm/yyyy) -not used at quote stage
